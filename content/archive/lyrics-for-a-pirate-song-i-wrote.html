This song was inspired by a class on American globalization, which covered the development of the seaborne economy of the 1600s and 1700s, as well as the scallywags who fought for democracy and freedom on the high seas (that was the authors' interpretation of piracy: a means to escape the crushing yoke of 'ye olde man').

*To be sung in a heavy pirate accent*
