The Call to Write

Dec 12, 2011
joseph campbell khaled allen warrior spirit

That was really awesome, to have someone specifically ask that I write for them.

So I’ve decided to do that. After all, if anything I am doing is bringing value to other people, I would like to contribute as much as possible.

That said, I realize Warrior Spirit needs to be more focused. In the coming weeks, I’ll be reworking the purpose of the blog, the article topics, and perhaps the writing style a little bit.

It will remain a blog about the path of self-discovery within the self, along the lines of Joseph Campbell’s mythic journey paradigm.

I’ll probably cut back to one or two articles a week, so I can keep up with my other projects and my new job in South Korea.

Thank you for the support, and I hope I can continue inspiring you to follow your dreams and grow your spirit.
